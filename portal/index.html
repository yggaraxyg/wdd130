ICE PROJECTS.

In Class Exercise, or ICE projects. In class, we are often assigned small-scale, short tasks to see how much work we can complete in an hour. Everything from mini-websites to social media posts fall under this category

BIG PROJECTS

This category is somewhere in between the ICE project and the WWR. there's no official category of projects like this, it's just whatever ICE projects we get that take a long time out of class to complete, or any multi-day projects that come along. Technically, THIS is one of the 'Big' projects.

WhiteWater Rafting

Ah, Whitewater Rafting, or WWR. WWR is a longer-term project we undertake over the course of the semester. we make a big project to start, and over time we add to it until we have a rather large project at the end of the semester (I think).
